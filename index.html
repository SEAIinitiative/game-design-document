Introduction
Summary / Pitch

In a dystopian future where traditional education has collapsed, knowledge is controlled by a corrupt organization known as the Knowledge Syndicate. Players, as young heroes, discover an ancient AI system, SEAI, hidden in the ruins of an old library. SEAI holds the key to reviving true education. Guided by SEAI, players embark on a journey to restore knowledge, battling the corrupt Syndicate and uncovering lost wisdom along the way. The game seamlessly integrates educational content into its engaging gameplay, offering a unique blend of learning and adventure.

Inspirations
  • Early Pok√©mon Games: Featuring a 16-bit style and top-down exploration that evokes nostalgia and simplicity.
  • Educational RPGs: Combining role-playing elements with educational content to enhance learning.
  • Classic Adventure Games: Drawing from timeless adventure mechanics and story-driven gameplay.
Player Experience

Players will:

  • Explore: Traverse diverse regions of Eduvia, each with unique educational themes and challenges.
  • Solve Puzzles: Engage with subject-related puzzles that reinforce critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Complete Quests: Undertake missions assigned by AI NPCs, each related to different educational subjects.
  • Knowledge-Based Duels: Engage in intellectual duels using knowledge acquired throughout the game.
  • Learn: Gain knowledge points to unlock new abilities and progress in the story.

The aim is to create an immersive and educational experience that is both enjoyable and intellectually stimulating.

Concept
Core Loop
  1. Explore: Traverse diverse regions of Eduvia, each offering unique challenges and quests.
  2. Solve Puzzles: Engage with educational puzzles to acquire knowledge.
  3. Complete Quests: Finish quests to gain knowledge points and progress the story.
  4. Record Knowledge: Use the Knowledge Journal to track and review acquired information.
  5. Progress: Unlock new regions and abilities by utilizing accumulated knowledge.
Themes
  • Knowledge vs. Ignorance: The struggle between enlightenment and control.
  • Discovery and Learning: The joy and power of uncovering new information.
  • Courage and Perseverance: The resilience needed to seek and defend knowledge.
Primary Mechanics
  • Exploration: Navigate through a 16-bit styled, top-down world filled with diverse environments.
  • Knowledge Acquisition: Collect knowledge points by completing educational tasks.
  • Puzzle Solving: Solve puzzles related to various subjects to advance in the game.
Secondary Mechanics
  • Inventory Management: Manage items, educational tools, and resources within a system inspired by Zelda BOTW.
  • Skill Trees: Unlock and enhance abilities through a structured progression system based on acquired knowledge.
Tertiary Mechanics
  • Mini-Games: Engage in educational mini-games that provide additional learning opportunities and rewards.
  • Crafting: Collect resources to craft educational tools and upgrade items that assist in quests.
Combat

Knowledge Duels: Engage in intellectual battles using the knowledge gained from quests and puzzles. These duels test the player's understanding and application of learned concepts rather than traditional combat skills.

Puzzles

Subject-Related Puzzles: Each puzzle is designed to teach and reinforce concepts from subjects such as math, science, history, and literature. Solving these puzzles provides knowledge points and other rewards.

Quests
  • Story-Driven Quests: Follow the main storyline by completing quests given by NPCs, each relating to different educational topics and historical contexts.
  • Side Quests: Additional missions that offer deeper insights into specific subjects and provide extra rewards.
AI / NPCs
  • Historical Figures: AI-driven characters modeled after historical figures and subject matter experts provide guidance, assign quests, and offer educational insights.
  • Mentors and Allies: Characters who assist the player throughout their journey, each with unique abilities and knowledge areas.
Special Systems
  • Token-Based Reward System: Earn tokens for completing quests and achieving milestones. These tokens can be used to unlock additional content, access premium features, and gain blockchain-based certificates.
  • Knowledge Journal: A Pokedex-like system that records all acquired knowledge. It serves as a reference guide and a note-taking tool, with AI assistance to help organize and review information.
Progression
  • Skill Unlocks: Gain new skills and abilities by earning knowledge points and completing quests.
  • Region Unlocks: Access new areas of Eduvia as the player progresses in the story and gathers more knowledge.
  • Achievements and Rewards: Earn badges, points, and tokens for educational accomplishments, encouraging continuous learning and mastery.

Art
Design
Regions

The visual design of the game's regions will reflect the diverse educational themes and environments in Eduvia. Each region will have its own unique aesthetic to visually communicate the subject matter and cultural background it represents.

  • Starting Village: A serene, technologically advanced community with vibrant colors, lush greenery, and modern architecture.
  • Library Ruins: Ancient, majestic ruins with overgrown foliage, intricate carvings, and remnants of a once-great civilization.
  • Tech City: A bustling metropolis with sleek, futuristic buildings, neon lights, and high-tech devices scattered around.
  • Forest of Myths: A mystical forest with towering trees, sparkling streams, and magical creatures. The palette includes deep greens, earthy browns, and ethereal glows.
  • Mountain of Wisdom: Majestic mountains with snow-capped peaks, ancient temples, and hidden caves. The design will include rugged terrain and serene, meditative spots.
Characters

Character design will maintain a consistent 16-bit style, creating a nostalgic yet fresh visual experience.

  • Protagonist: A young, adventurous scholar with a customizable appearance, allowing players to personalize their character.
  • SEAI: A holographic entity with a sleek, futuristic design, appearing as a floating orb or an ethereal figure that changes colors when conveying different types of knowledge.
  • Knowledge Syndicate Leaders: Distinctive, imposing figures with dark, elaborate costumes that reflect their control over knowledge.
  • Mentor Figure: An elderly, wise character with gentle features and traditional scholarly attire.
  • Rival: A dynamic, conflicted character with a more rugged appearance, transitioning from antagonist to ally.
  • Allies: Diverse characters representing various educational disciplines, each with unique outfits and accessories that signify their expertise.

Visual effects will enhance the immersive experience of the game, making knowledge acquisition and puzzle-solving visually rewarding.

Dynamic lighting will play a crucial role in creating an immersive and atmospheric game world.

  • Directional Lighting: Emphasizing the time of day and weather conditions in each region to add realism and depth to the environment.
  • Interactive Lighting: Lighting that responds to player actions, such as torches lighting up dark areas, mystical glows revealing hidden paths, and spotlights highlighting important objects.
  • Special Effects Lighting: Dramatic lighting changes during significant events, such as the discovery of SEAI, confrontations with the Knowledge Syndicate, and moments of critical knowledge acquisition.

Game Experience
UI / UX

The user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design of SEAI are critical to ensuring players can easily navigate the game and access its educational content. Inspired by the clean and intuitive designs of games like Zelda: Breath of the Wild, our goal is to create a seamless and engaging experience.

  • Intuitive and Accessible Interface: The UI will be designed to be user-friendly, with clear icons, readable text, and straightforward navigation.
    • Inventory Screen: A visually organized grid system for managing items, similar to Zelda: BotW, allowing players to easily sort, select, and use items.
    • Quest Log: A dedicated section for tracking active and completed quests, with detailed descriptions and objectives.
    • Knowledge Points Display: A clear indicator of earned knowledge points and their allocation.
    • Knowledge Acquisition System: Similar to the Pok√©dex, this system will record acquired knowledge and serve as a note-taking tool with AI assistance.
Controls

The control scheme will be designed to be simple and responsive, ensuring that players can effortlessly interact with the game world. Controls will be adapted for different platforms, including PC, mobile, and console.

  • Exploration: Smooth and fluid movement controls for navigating the top-down world.
    • PC: Arrow keys/WASD for movement, mouse for interaction.
    • Mobile: Touch controls for movement and interactions.
    • Console: Analog stick for movement, buttons for actions.
  • Interactions: Easy-to-use controls for interacting with NPCs, objects, and environments.
    • PC: E key or left mouse button for interactions.
    • Mobile: Tap for interactions.
    • Console: A button for interactions.
Menus

The menu system will be designed for clarity and ease of use, enabling players to quickly access and manage game settings, inventory, and quests.

  • Main Menu: Provides access to game settings, save/load options, and exit game.
  • Inventory Menu: Organized into categories such as items, equipment, and knowledge artifacts.
  • Quest Menu: Lists active, completed, and available quests with detailed information.
  • Settings Menu: Options for adjusting audio, video, controls, and other preferences.
  • Knowledge Acquisition System Menu: Allows players to review acquired knowledge, access notes, and utilize AI for enhanced learning.
